# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:
- Being respectful and constructive.
- Giving and accepting feedback in good faith.
- Prioritizing collaboration and empathy.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruptive or bad-faith communication.

## Enforcement

Report incidents to: socialtleea@gmail.com

Project maintainers are responsible for reviewing and enforcing this code of conduct.
